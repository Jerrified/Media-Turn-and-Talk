Batman (Bruce Wayne)

Batman originated from an incident in Bruce's childhood; after witnessing the murder of his parents Dr. Thomas Wayne and Martha Wayne, he swore vengeance against criminals, an oath tempered by a sense of justice. Bruce trains himself physically and intellectually and crafts a bat-inspired persona to fight crime.

But, It's BATman!!

Batman has a multitude of “powers”.

  • He has money. This money gives him influence around the world, the ability of innovation, freely create any gadget or piece he requires and he has multiple companies at his disposal.
  • He’s well trained. He’s mastered over 120 forms of martial arts, can survive minutes underwater, is able to lift 700lbs and can heal quicker than the average human and sleep less. Why? He meditates. Ra’s Al Ghul taught him everything about fighting with more than fists. With your mind, soul and surroundings.
  • He’s a symbol too. This symbol or emblem gives him the power of notoriety. Ergo, people will hear or see a bat and automatically assume he’s nearby.
  • Resourcefulness. Expanding on the second half of the second point, Batman uses his surroundings in many ways. Coupled with his detective skills, contingencies and “paranoia”, he’s known to always understand and learn about the field he’s about to walk into. He makes it his business to recognise EVERY character in play and every object in sight. Using smoke pellets, his cape, other opponents and even trickery on a verbal, psychological and mental scale (most notably with the Joker).
  • Paranoia. Again, expanding on the previous point. His paranoia allows him to always be ready for a scenario, no matter how ridiculous. In case Superman goes berserk? Gold and green Kryptonite. Green Lanterns? The colour yellow. The Flash? A bomb that, no matter what speed, goes off if he stops running. Martian Manhunter? Fire. The list goes on. His contingencies and paranoia are spotlighted in the known comic arc Justice League: Tower of Babel.
  • Intelligence. Batman has degrees in multiple fields. Forensics, criminology, physics, biology, chemistry, computer science, psychology and more. He’s also the renowned “Greatest Detective” and this is further enhanced by his extensive database, social circle and money.
